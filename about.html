About Tomotina

A nurturing child development centre built around every child’s potential.

Tomotina Kids Development Centre supports children with autism and diverse developmental needs through therapy, education, skill-building, sports-based activities, and strong family partnership in a calm, structured, and inclusive environment.

Who we are

A place where support is individualized, structured, and compassionate.

Tomotina Kids Development Centre is dedicated to helping children build communication, confidence, independence, and meaningful life skills through specialized support and a research-informed, child-centered approach.

We work with children who may need support with autism, ADHD, learning difficulties, speech and language delays, behavioural challenges, and sensory processing needs.

Vision and mission

What guides our work

Everything we do is shaped by inclusion, meaningful support, and long-term growth.

Our Vision

To create an inclusive environment where every child is empowered to reach their full potential and grow with confidence, happiness, and independence.

Our Mission

  • Provide high-quality therapeutic and educational support
  • Encourage independence and life skills
  • Support families through guidance and collaboration
  • Promote holistic child development
