About Tomotina

Support that sees the child, not just the diagnosis

We support children with autism, ADHD, learning difficulties, speech and language delays, behavioural challenges, and sensory processing needs.

Thoughtfully designed for meaningful progress

At Tomotina, every child is supported through individualized intervention, structured activities, and a holistic developmental approach that nurtures communication, social, emotional, and life skills.

  • Autism Spectrum Disorder support
  • Speech & language development
  • Occupational and behavioural support
  • Academic and school-readiness guidance
  • Sports therapy and daily living skills
